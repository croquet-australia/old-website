2011 ACA AGM

We wish to advise that the 62nd  Annual General Meeting of the Australian Croquet Association Incorporated will be held on Monday 28th March 2011 in Tasmania.  The ACA executive meeting will be held on Friday 25 March and the Presidents meeting on Saturday 26 March with venues to be advised.

Nominations for positions for the ACA will close on the 27 November 2010, all nominations must be in writing and signed by the nominee and be forwarded to the ED ACA on or before 27 November 2010. (Nomination Form - Word, PDF).

If you have questions please do not hesitate to contact me on 0400 385 687.

Gail Farr
Executive Director
