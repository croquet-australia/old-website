Changes To Referee Regulations

The ACA wishes to advise that as of the 15 February 2011 the ACA will adopt the proposed changes to the regulations for referees as advised by the I.L.C.

Please find attached a copy of the changes and a circular advising how the changes will impact on the regulations. We also wish to advise:

To avoid the numbering problem we retain the R prefix and adopt them as part of the Australian TRs, deleting the existing ones on Refereeing by replacing their text with "Reserved".
