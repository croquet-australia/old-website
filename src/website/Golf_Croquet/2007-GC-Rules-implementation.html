Implementation of the 2007 Golf Croquet Rules

The WCF released the following with the 2007 Golf Croquet Rules:

Note to WCF Associations and players

1. The WCF Rules of Golf Croquet 2007 may be reproduced by WCF member associations in any format they consider suitable, at their own cost, for re-sale to others, provided the reproduction acknowledges the copyright of the World Croquet Federation.

2. The WCF Rules of Golf Croquet 2007 may be reproduced by members of the public, at their own cost, for their own personal use, provided the reproduction acknowledges the copyright of the World Croquet Federation.

3. WCF member associations may begin to use these Rules in any event, of which they are the governing body, at any time in the future, from 4th June 2007.

4. WCF events will not use the WCF Rules of Golf Croquet 2007 until after 1st January 2008.

5. For clarity, The WCF European Team Championship in June 2007 and the 2nd WCF Women’s World Golf Croquet Championship in September 2007 will be played to the 2005 version. The 8th WCF World Golf Croquet Championship in February 2008 will be played to this 2007 version.

Please note that the ACA have decided to implement the Rules immediately (point 3 above).
