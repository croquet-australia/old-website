Variants

  • Malletball and Toequet: are new giant-sized soccer ball wicket games. These games, played with soccer balls and oversized
    wickets, were created by The Toequet Company of Terre Haute, Indiana, and developed and promoted at the National Croquet Center in West Palm Beach, Florida. They combine the fun of Backyard Croquet with the spirit of Extreme Croquet. Everyone can play, both adults and kids. Just about anytime. Almost anywhere.
  • Cruol is a lawn game that employs croquet mallets and balls. It is a hybrid of croquet and billiards.
  • San Francisco Extreme Croquet Club.
  • Roque. A little information and some possible contacts.
  • Wicket Grounds Croquet Club. Play and sponsor individual one ball style using both six and nine wickets with a recognizable combination of USCA rules. In the last three years events have run in three states and drawn many people from the backyards of New England onto actual competiton lawns without the stress of partner play. See the site to find out about forthcoming events.
