2002 National Handicap & Golf Croquet Results


National Handicap Tournament

This year's tournament was held at Rich River on 19 - 25 October 2002. The entries were limited to 64, as this was considered the maximum that could be handled with the local facilities. The results are as follows :

Handicap Singles

Block winners; Ian Bassett Vic; Ann Crow Vic; Fred Lane SA; Russ Shellie Vic; Phyl Woollard SA; Neil Bonnar SA; Alan Richardson Vic; Max Huddle NSW.

Winner:Ann Crow
Runner Up; Max Huddle,

Handicap Doubles

Block winners; I Bassett - P Woollard; A Crow - E Lieschke; J Evans - W Lane; J Makepeace - B Drilling; V Jones - A Richardson; M Gunn - M Huddle; G Hindhaugh - B Buddle; N Gardner - E Irlam.

Winners:Joy Makepeace - Bill Drilling
Runners Up; Nigel Gardner - Eileen Irlam.
